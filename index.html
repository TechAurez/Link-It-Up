Link It Up

Where Clues Connect & Fun Collects!

⚡ General Rules

  • Teams: The event is for teams of two.
  • Fair Play: Cheating is not allowed. Be a good sport!
  • Judges' Decision: The judges' decisions are final.
  • Points: The team with the highest score at the end of all three rounds wins.

🎯 Round 1: Picture Connection

Your team will see a set of 3-4 images. Find the common link or connection between them.

Examples: The link could be a movie title, brand, celebrity, famous phrase, etc.

  • ⏱ Time Limit: 15 seconds per question
  • ✅ Correct Answer: +10 points
  • ↪ Pass: +5 points (strategic choice!)

🎵 Round 2: Sound Clues & Live Act

Part A: Sound Clues

Guess the connection from short audio clips (songs, dialogues, jingles, etc.).

Time: 15 seconds per clue

Part B: Live Act

Our volunteers perform dumb charades/gestures. You must guess the connection!

Time: 30 seconds per act

  • ✅ Scoring same as Round 1

🌀 Round 3: Classroom Maze Craze

One teammate is blindfolded and must navigate a maze made of desks & chairs.

Their partner gives directions using funny sounds only (no words!).

  • "Meow" = Left
  • "Woof" = Right
  • "Quack" = Straight

Disqualification: If words are used instead of sounds.

🎭 Checkpoints with Funny Tasks

  • 😂 Laughing Stop - Fake laugh loudly for 5 seconds
  • 🍌 Banana Walk - Hold a book on head for 5 steps
  • 🔡 Funny Talk - Say the alphabet backward
  • 😀 Emoji Act - Act out an emoji given by the judge
